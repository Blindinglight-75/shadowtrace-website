About ShadowTrace

Bringing transparency to blockchain

We build tools that help investigators, compliance teams, and regulators understand what's happening on public blockchains.

Our Mission

Blockchain technology promised transparency, but the reality is more complex. Pseudonymous addresses, cross-chain bridges, and privacy protocols create layers of obfuscation that can be exploited by bad actors.

We believe that investigators shouldn't need to be blockchain experts to follow the money. Our mission is to make blockchain intelligence accessible, actionable, and evidence-grade.

ShadowTrace exists to give the people protecting our financial systems the tools they need to do their jobs effectively.
