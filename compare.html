Common questions

How does ShadowTrace data coverage compare?

We cover 40+ blockchains including all major networks (Bitcoin, Ethereum, Tron, BSC, Polygon, Solana, etc.) and continuously add new chains based on investigative demand. For most investigations, this covers 95%+ of transaction volume.

Can I migrate from another platform?

Yes. We support case migration and can work alongside your existing tools during a transition period. Many teams run ShadowTrace in parallel before fully switching.

Is ShadowTrace accepted in legal proceedings?

Our reports are designed to be evidence-grade, with full audit trails and explainable methodology. We've supported investigations that have progressed through UK and international courts.

What if I need features you don't have yet?

We actively build based on customer feedback. As a growing platform, we're able to prioritise features that matter to your team — something larger vendors rarely offer.
