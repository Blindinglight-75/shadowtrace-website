Investigation workflow

1. Ingest & Index

Start with a wallet address, transaction hash, or entity name. ShadowTrace instantly retrieves on-chain data from our indexed database covering Bitcoin, Ethereum, and major EVM chains—with historical depth back to genesis blocks.

2. Investigate & Trace

Use our interactive graph canvas to expand transaction paths, cluster related wallets, and identify fund flows. Annotate entities, tag addresses of interest, and collaborate with team members in shared case workspaces.

3. Score & Explain

Every address and transaction receives an explainable risk score. Drill down to see exactly which factors contributed—direct exposure, behavioural patterns, counterparty risk—so you can justify findings to stakeholders.

4. Report & Export

Generate evidence-grade PDF reports with full methodology documentation, timestamped screenshots, and reproducible audit trails. Export data to CSV or JSON for integration with case management systems.

04 // SECURITY

Enterprise security for sensitive investigations.

ShadowTrace is built to meet the security requirements of law enforcement agencies and regulated financial institutions. We maintain SOC 2 Type II certification and operate in compliance with ISO 27001 standards.

All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Access is controlled through role-based permissions with full audit logging. Enterprise clients can configure SSO/SAML integration, data retention policies, and custom deployment options.
